About MBA

At MBA, we understand that navigating family law matters requires more than legal expertise—it demands compassion, discretion, and unwavering advocacy. Our family law attorneys combine meticulous attention to detail with a client-centered approach designed to reduce stress during life's most sensitive transitions. A practice built for sensitive decisions

We founded MBA to create a different kind of family law practice—one that balances legal authority with genuine warmth. What guides our work

Every agreement, filing, and legal document is crafted with meticulous attention to detail. Thorough preparation protects your rights and prevents costly complications down the road.
